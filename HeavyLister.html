Jamie Foster

Heavy Lister




Date: Winter 2020-21
Project: Design Solution to Food Inequity
Team: Jamie Foster, Leah Raftis, Gagan Dhaliwal, Nicole Visentin, Stuti Duggal
In a two phase agile sprint design method, we conducted research around food inequity and possible solutions. My team designed an app to save users time and money by comparing their shopping lists across different grocery stores for the best price, accounting for brands, coupons and even gas mileage.

I focused mostly on the prototyping and wireframes, and became very familiar with Figma. This project's scope allowed me to really see multiple design iterations and I enjoyed watching each deliverable come out better than the last.

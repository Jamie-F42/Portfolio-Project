Which Cosplay Class are You?

Written by: Jamie
November 25, 2025
5 minutes

Cosplaying is a fun and creative way to embody your favourite characters and engage with other fans. Whether you specialize in craft, in act or just looking damn good there is a place for you at the cos-table. Roll your stats and pick a class! There’s so many ways to cosplay your favourite characters; how do you create your final form?


Con Mom

Like a shepherd you protect and guide your flock. You’re probably the most experienced with conventions in your group. You know the best booths, when to line up for the freebies, the people to avoid and how much water to drink–ALL OF IT.

Speed 4
Attention 8
Stamina 6
Specialty
Second Rodeo: immune to confusion and lost effects
Inventory
water, granola bars, tote bag, convention map, phone charger, more water


Pro Cosplayer

Armour-dremmeled. Wig-styled. Colour contacts-drying your eyes out. You stayed up late all week to get this costume done and it looks amazing. As a jack-of-all-trades you made everything yourself, wig styling, costume makeup, the foam armour and even sewed the fabric pieces. You are an artiste and love the craft- wearing it to the con is just displaying your hard work.

Speed 6
Attention 7
Stamina 2
Specialty
Master Repair: can fix any broken piece instantly
Inventory
costume repair kit, bag that's part of the costume, hairspray, so many photographer’s cards


Niche Cosplayer

You have perfectly embodied your favorite character from that anime or video game that no one is recognizing–and you don't care. You feel great, and while the lack of recognition doesn’t feel great at times, being able to move through the con floor without being stopped for pictures all the time is nice. And when that person does come up recognizing who you are it feels amazing you’ve made an instant friend.

Speed 9
Attention 6
Stamina 4
Specialty
Recognize: can creat an instant bond once per long rest
Inventory
money for that specific fandom merch, safety pins, nail glue, sweet treats


Purchaser

You don’t have much interest in the making, but you love the wearing. You like to look good and dress as your favourite characters making friends. There is nothing wrong with buying your cosplay, don’t let the haters shame you. They are just jealous you have slept well.

Speed 7
Attention 5
Stamina 9
Specialty
Well Rested: can take two actions per turn
Inventory
already loads of stickers and pins, wig comb, no money


Handler

Maybe you have a casual clothes costume on, but probably none at all. You are probably a partner of a cosplayer or part of a friend group that cosplays but you usually don't. Unfortunately you are the handler;holder of stuff. You are the agile team member able to move through crowds easily, you run the errands, get their food and help with photoshoots. They appreciate you, even if they don’t tell you enough and they own you a hearty cocktail.

Speed 10
Attention 8
Stamina 3
Specialty
Helping Hand: give all party members +1 to all stats
Inventory
everyone's wallets and water bottles, extra bags, the books and posters you came for

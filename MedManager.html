Jamie Foster

MED Manager




Date: Fall 2020
Project: Personal Health Assistance Solution
Team: Jamie Foster, Jihye Han, Gagan Dhaliwal, Yinglu Zhang
Objectives:
  • Simple Design
  • Hassle-Free Communication
  • Manageable Records
  • Ensure Confidence
My team was tasked with creating a personal health solution. We designated our own problem space of low-English proficiency speakers, and came up with a solution to help them manage their own health and navigate medical visits with professionals who do not speak their native language.

This project was my first big step into turning research and ideas into an actual design. I got to focus on prototyping and conducting tests with participants on our early wireframes to discover issues and improve on our designs.
